Car Quiz

How to Play:

  1. Click on the "Start the Quiz" button to begin the game.
  2. You'll be presented with a series of multiple-choice questions related to cars, Formula 1, car brands and many other.
  3. Read each question carefully and select the answer you believe is correct.
  4. After selecting an answer, you'll receive immediate feedback on whether it was correct or incorrect.
  5. At the end of the quiz, your final score will be displayed.

Tips:

  • Take your time and read each question carefully.
  • If you're unsure of an answer, make an educated guess.
  • Learn from your mistakes by reviewing the feedback provided for incorrect answers.
  • Enjoy the quiz and have fun!
